# Top 10 Global Education Companies to Study for Upwise

## 1. Khan Academy / Khanmigo

**Why it matters:** Gold standard for trust + mastery learning + AI used responsibly.

**What to study:**
- Skill trees
- Mastery progression
- Parent trust positioning
- AI that guides instead of gives answers

Khan Academy remains one of the world's most widely used learning platforms.

**Upwise takeaway:** Build trust first, AI second.

## 2. Alpha School

**Why it matters:** Most disruptive modern learning model.

**What to study:**
- 2-hour focused academics
- Personalised AI pathways
- Rewards + ownership
- "Students move at their pace"

Alpha claims strong test outcomes and accelerated progress.

**Upwise takeaway:** Parents want efficiency.

## 3. Duolingo

**Why it matters:** Best gamification engine in education.

**What to study:**
- Streaks
- XP points
- Notifications
- Habit loops
- Cute brand voice

**Upwise takeaway:** Learning must feel sticky.

## 4. Photomath

**Why it matters:** Huge scale because it solves a real pain point instantly.

**What to study:**
- Instant feedback
- Step-by-step support
- Mobile simplicity

Photomath remains a leading maths support tool.

**Upwise takeaway:** Reduce frustration fast.

## 5. IXL Learning

**Why it matters:** Strong curriculum alignment + relentless practice model.

**What to study:**
- Diagnostic testing
- Personal recommendations
- Parent dashboards
- Curriculum coverage

**Upwise takeaway:** Parents buy measurable progress.

## 6. Kumon

**Why it matters:** Traditional giant.

**What to study:**
- Habit formation
- Parent discipline psychology
- Franchise scale

**Upwise takeaway:** Repetition sells, but can be modernised.

## 7. Thinkster

**Why it matters:** Human tutor + AI hybrid model.

**What to study:**
- Blended support
- Accountability coaching
- Messaging around confidence

**Upwise takeaway:** Human support layer may outperform pure AI.

## 8. Quizlet

**Why it matters:** Massive student engagement.

**What to study:**
- Micro-learning
- Self-testing
- Fast wins

**Upwise takeaway:** Kids need short bursts, not long lectures.

## 9. BYJU'S

**Why it matters:** Huge growth story, plus cautionary tale.

**What to study:**
- Scale marketing
- Parent aspiration selling
- What happens when growth outruns product

**Upwise takeaway:** Product quality > hype.

## 10. Outschool

**Why it matters:** Parents pay for enrichment + interest-led learning.

**What to study:**
- Community trust
- Flexible learning
- Niche demand creation

**Upwise takeaway:** Not all value is tutoring.
